About

Background and direction

I am completing a Master of Business Analytics with a focus on Supply Chain Management. My work so far has involved data transformation, reporting, and working with systems where data and operations meet.

Before this, I spent time working across agricultural environments in both Australia and New Zealand. That experience gave me a more practical understanding of how operations actually run — and how small inefficiencies can create larger impacts.

More recently, I have contributed to improving data workflows using Microsoft Fabric, which strengthened my interest in roles that combine data, systems thinking, and real operational improvement.

Outside of work, I’m drawn to nature and calm environments, which reflects in how I approach problems — with a preference for clarity, balance, and thoughtful structure.
